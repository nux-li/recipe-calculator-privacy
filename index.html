Privacy Policy

Recipe Calculator · Last updated 24 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Recipe Calculator (“the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We have designed Recipe Calculator to collect as little personal data as possible. The App does not require an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or any directly identifying information.

The following limited data is collected automatically:

a) Advertising Identifier (AAID)

When you see advertisements in the App, Google AdMob collects your device’s Android Advertising ID and basic device information (device model, operating system version, language, approximate region based on IP address). This is used to deliver advertisements and to limit how often you see the same ad. You can reset or disable this identifier at any time in your Android settings under “Privacy” or “Ads”.

b) Anonymous Purchase Data

If you purchase the “Remove Ads” lifetime upgrade, our payment processor RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier generated by your device, the date and amount of the purchase, the transaction ID issued by Google Play, and your country (derived from your Google Play account). This data is used to validate your purchase and restore it on devices where you sign in with the same Google account.

c) Locally Stored Preferences

The App stores your selected language, your most recent units, and your purchase status on your device using local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

What we do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or other directly identifying information
  • Recipes, ingredients, conversion history, or any content you type into the App
  • Your precise location
  • Photos, contacts, microphone, or camera data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial account details (Google Play handles all payment information)

2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Display advertisements (only for users who have not purchased the “Remove Ads” upgrade)
  • Validate and restore in-app purchases across your devices
  • Comply with applicable consumer protection and tax laws

3. Third Parties That Receive Data

We share data with the following third parties strictly to operate the App:

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC) — serves advertisements and provides anonymized analytics. AdMob privacy policy
  • Google User Messaging Platform (Google LLC) — handles consent for personalized advertising in the EU, UK, and Switzerland. Google privacy policy
  • RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.) — processes and validates in-app purchases. RevenueCat privacy policy
  • Google Play (Google LLC) — handles all payment transactions. Google Play privacy policy

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any other third party.

4. Consent for Advertising (EU, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be shown a consent dialog when you first open the App. You may choose between personalized advertising (which uses your Advertising ID and approximate region) and non-personalized advertising (which uses no identifiers). You can change this choice at any time by opening the App and tapping the “Privacy options” link below the language selector.

5. Data Retention

  • Advertising data: retained by Google AdMob for the duration described in their privacy policy, typically up to 14 months. You may delete it sooner by resetting your Advertising ID in Android settings.
  • Purchase data: retained by RevenueCat and Google Play for the lifetime of your account, in order to honor restorations of your “Remove Ads” purchase. This is required by law in many jurisdictions.
  • Local preferences: retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data manually.

6. Children’s Privacy

Recipe Calculator is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us at the email below and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the GDPR in the EU/UK and the CCPA in California), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below. Please note that because the App does not collect directly identifying information, we may need additional information from you to locate any data tied to your device.

8. Data Transfers

Some of the third parties listed above (Google, RevenueCat) are based in the United States. By using the App, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other jurisdictions, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence. These transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent legal mechanisms.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page indicates when it was last changed. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
